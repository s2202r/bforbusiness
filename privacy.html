Privacy policy

Last updated: 18 May 2026

This privacy policy explains how BUILTFORBUSINESS LLP ("we", "us", "our") handles personal information collected through this website (bfor.business) and the corporate communication channels listed on it. Each of our products (CareLink, EPR HQ, Divyam, Saathi and others) publishes its own product-specific privacy policy. Those product policies govern data collected via those apps and prevail over this notice for product-specific data.

1. Who we are

BUILTFORBUSINESS LLP is a Limited Liability Partnership incorporated in India under the LLP Act, 2008. Our LLPIN is ACQ-7687. Our registered office is Flat 1804, Tower B1, Supertech Eco Village 1, Bisrakh, Gautam Buddha Nagar, Uttar Pradesh 201306, India.

2. Information we collect on this website

This website is largely static and does not require you to create an account. We collect personal information only when you choose to provide it — for example, by writing to us at the email addresses listed on the contact page. In that case, we receive your email address, name (if you include it), and the content of your message.

Our hosting provider (Vercel) and DNS provider may automatically log standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, referring page, and timestamp for security and operational purposes.

3. How we use information

  • To respond to enquiries you send to us;
  • To maintain and secure the operation of this website;
  • To comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests from government authorities.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use this website's data for targeted advertising or for training third-party AI models.

4. Sharing

We share information only with: (a) service providers that help us operate this website (such as hosting and email providers) under contractual confidentiality obligations; and (b) authorities, when required by law, legal process, or to protect rights and safety.

5. Retention

Correspondence is retained for as long as needed to resolve your enquiry and to keep an audit trail consistent with applicable Indian law. Server logs are retained for up to 90 days unless required for longer to investigate an incident.

6. Your rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), you have the right to access, correct, and request erasure of your personal data held by us, and to nominate another person to exercise these rights on your behalf in the event of your death or incapacity. You can also withdraw consent and lodge a grievance with us.

7. Grievance officer

To exercise your rights or raise a complaint, write to:

Grievance Officer, BUILTFORBUSINESS LLP founder@bfor.business
We will acknowledge within 7 days and respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We use industry-standard transport encryption (HTTPS), access controls, and reputable infrastructure providers. No system is perfectly secure; please use strong, unique passwords for any product accounts and notify us promptly if you suspect unauthorised activity.

9. Children

This website is not directed at children under 18. Our products may have their own age policies described in their respective product privacy policies.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be signalled by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

11. Contact

For privacy questions about this website, write to founder@bfor.business.
